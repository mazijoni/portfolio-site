01 Me

About me

"Building games,
apps, and tools —
one commit at a time." — Jonatan Lund Ermesjø

Hey — I'm Jonatan, a creative developer who loves turning ideas into interactive experiences. I specialize in 3D game development using Godot, with a particular passion for crafting horror games that keep players on the edge of their seats.

When I'm not building immersive worlds, you'll find me exploring web-based projects, experimenting with new tech, or diving into games myself — because playing is just as important as making.

I blend creativity and technical skill to deliver experiences that are fun, engaging, and a little bit spooky. I operate under the name Maze Development — my personal studio where I ship everything from Godot games to custom browsers.
